My fan site

Starhome Sprinter is a Flash animated Internet cartoon. It mixes surreal humour with references to 1980s and 1990s pop culture, notably video games, classic television and popular music.

Most of the site's traffic comes from the United States; events in the cartoon itself usually take place in Free Place, USA.

The cartoons are nominally centered on title character Starhome Sprinter. However, the series titled Muscle Man Email, in which another main character, Muscle Man, answers emails from viewers, is the most popular and prominent feature of the site.

Starhome's theme song says, "Everyperson! Everyperson!"
